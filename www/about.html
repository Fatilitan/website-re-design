About FightIQ Smart Martial Arts

FightIQ is a Mixed Martial Arts gym based in Amsterdam for aspiring MMA fighters, grapplers, kickboxers, or anyone looking to learn self-defense. Our gym features an extensive coaching staff with experience at the highest of levels.

Mixed Martial Arts is a combat sport allowing striking and grappling. In the octagon, you can expect anything. So during training, we analyze the movements and skills of our students to help them get ready with the best strategic fight plan.

We’re making sure that both standing and ground techniques are mastered. Along with the aid of our fantastic coaching staff, we want you to succeed. Additionally, we frequently invite the best MMA and Grappling competitors in the world to share the newest developments in the game, and help our team reach our goals!

If training in a smart mixed martial arts gym interests you, have a look at our schedule and contact us to reserve your free trial! See you in our octagon at FightIQ Amsterdam.

We are located at Van Marwijk Kooystraat 4, 1096 BR Amsterdam, just off the Overamstel metro station. Feel free to call us at +31 06 57504080.
