THE TRAINERS

FightIQ’s coaches have got you covered with all the bases for Martial arts. Providing diverse trainings throughout each week, we are delighted to have an elite and enthusiastic coaching staff.

Meet FightIQ’s coaches and come learn from them:

Donovan Panayiotis

MMA Coach

Not only is Donavan the founder and owner of FightIQ, but he was a Hit Fighting Championship fighter and MMA champion from the Netherlands. In addition to his impressive profile, MMA Record, pro and amateur fights, he’s ready to teach you all you need to know to become a Pro.

Mohamed Elawil

Kickboxing Coach

Mo is currently a professional MMA fighter for UAE Warriors. He is set to challenge for the UAE lightweight belt sometime soon. Firstly, Mo understands striking and clinching very well, especially in relation to MMA. Secondly, he is a highly skilled competitor which translates very well to his coaching. Come learn from one of the best!

Emad Ahmedin

No GI BJJ

Emad is a 10th Planet system instructor. Moreover, he is a high level grappler earning the ADCC Netherlands gold medal, Amsterdam BJJ Open gold medal, as well as the Dutch submission-only gold medal. The FightIQ family is delighted to have Emad join us as a coach.

George Ramm

Wrestling

George Ramm is one of our 3 wrestling coaches, specifically focusing on freestyle wrestling. George, began wrestling at the age of 11, meeting his coach Eddie Kavana, and has gone on to become a multiple English and British champion. He comes from a wrestling family. It should be noted his father, Jason Ramm, won a silver medal in the Wrestling European Championships.

Kansu Ildem

Greco-Roman Wrestling

Kansu is our Greco-Roman wrestling coach. First and foremost, he is a fantastic wrestler comepting at the international and national level for olympic wrestling. Above all, he is a great coach at teaching the original Olympic sport!

Adam Al Kandoussi

Wrestling

Adam is one of our 3 wrestling coaches. Another stellar coach, he placed 3rd at the 2017 European Pankration Championships. In addition, he is also on the Dutch freestyle wrestling team. Come learn wrestling from him every tuesday!

BamBam

Shoot-Boxing Coach

Coach BamBam is a slick and punishing striker who has more importantly coached for many years. Join him in learning the fundamentals of shoot-boxing and technique every Monday. Without a doubt, you won’t want to miss it.

Massimo Cappelli

MMA coach

Last but not least, Massimo coaches MMA every friday. With a Muay Thai background, Massimo undertands and teaches the MT clinch expertly, as well as conditioning, striking and other facets of MMA. Join him every friday for a challenging and intense class!

Interested? Register and come learn from them yourself.
